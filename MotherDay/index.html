Dear Mom

Every journey begins with a spark. It may struggle at first, searching for what it wants, but once it finds it, it will be able to grow and glow into something much more. Soon, all will see this fiery performance and be amazed. Every hero’s journey starts somewhere. It doesn’t matter where they start; they just need to start. Not every hero knows what they must do; some must search out their destiny. When they do find what they are destined for, they will begin to excel far more than anyone, even they themselves, thought they would, and even what seems to be the peak of their skill, they will rise. Soon, they will be the talk of the town; everyone will hear of this great person and their achievements, and knowing what they are becoming, they will continue and search out for his skills to be used elsewhere. When they find that place, they will continue to impress those around them and they will make great achievements. They will become known for their great achievements worldwide, but it all starts with a simple spark next to the fuel they need.
